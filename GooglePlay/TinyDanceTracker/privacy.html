2.Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide to us

When you register and use the Application, we may collect:

  • Account information (such as display name and phone number) provided through authentication and onboarding

When you use the Application, we and our third-party service providers may automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system, language, country)
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address (used for coarse location and fraud prevention)
  • Application usage data (features used, sessions, error reports)
  • Push notification token (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID / AAID), where applicable

2.3 Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect precise GPS location
  • We do not use artificial intelligence (AI) technologies to process your data
  • We do not collect biometric data
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties

3.How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Application's features (kick tracking, sharing with viewers, pregnancy progress)
  • Authenticate you and secure your account
  • Send push notifications you have opted into (e.g., kick alerts shared with followers)
  • Communicate with you about important notices and service updates
  • Display advertisements (see Section 6 — Advertising)
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4.Legal basis for processing (GDPR Article 6)

If you are in the EU, EEA, UK, or Switzerland, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Providing core App features (kick tracking, sharing) — Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): processing is necessary to provide the service you signed up for.
  • Sending push notifications — Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): you opt in at runtime.
  • Personalized advertising — Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): captured via the in-app consent dialog.
  • Non-personalized advertising — Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)): to fund the operation of the Application.
  • Fraud prevention and security — Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)).
  • Service communications — Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • Compliance with legal obligations — Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)).

You may withdraw consent at any time (see Section 10 — Your rights).

6.Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the Application. Advertising revenue helps us cover the operating costs of running the service (cloud infrastructure, push notifications, authentication).

6.1 Data collected by AdMob and its advertising partners

AdMob and its partners may collect and process:

  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • IP address (used for approximate location and fraud prevention)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, country)
  • App usage signals (ad views, clicks, time spent)
  • Approximate location derived from IP

6.2 Purpose of advertising data processing

  • Serving advertisements (personalized or non-personalized)
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Frequency capping (so you do not see the same ad too often)
  • Fraud and abuse prevention

6.3 Personalized vs. non-personalized advertising

  • Personalized ads use information about your device and activity to show ads more relevant to you. These require your consent in the EU, EEA, UK, and Switzerland.
  • Non-personalized ads are based only on contextual information, such as the content of the App and approximate location at country level.

6.4 Consent for users in the EU, EEA, UK, and Switzerland

If you are located in the EU, EEA, UK, or Switzerland, before any advertisements are shown, we will present you with a consent dialog implemented using Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) and based on the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework (TCF v2.2). The dialog lets you:

  • Accept personalized advertising
  • Reject personalized advertising (you may then see non-personalized ads, or no ads, depending on your choices)
  • Manage which advertising vendors may process your data

You can change your choice at any time via Settings → Privacy options within the Application.

6.5 Other ways to control advertising

  • Reset or delete your advertising ID: On your Android device, go to Settings → Google → Ads and select "Delete advertising ID" or "Reset advertising ID".
  • Remove Ads (upcoming): A future version of the Application will offer a "Remove Ads" in-app purchase that disables advertising entirely. When available, this option will appear in the App's Settings.

6.6 Target audience

The Application is not directed at children. Our AdMob settings are configured accordingly.

7.International data transfers

The Application uses Google Firebase and Google AdMob, both operated by Google LLC, which may process and store your data outside your country of residence — including in the United States.

Where data is transferred from the EU, EEA, UK, or Switzerland to a country not deemed to provide an adequate level of data protection, the transfer is safeguarded by the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) entered into between Google and its customers, as described in Google's data processing terms.
